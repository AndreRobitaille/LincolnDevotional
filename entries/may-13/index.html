May 13

Mutual Candour

Scripture

KJV

Judge not, that ye be not judged. And why beholdest thou the mote that is in thy brother’s eye, but considerest not the beam that is in thine own eye?

ESV

“Judge not, that you be not judged. Why do you see the speck that is in your brother’s eye, but do not notice the log that is in your own eye?

Matthew 7:1, 3

Poem

Make us, by thy transforming grace,
Great Saviour, daily more like thee;
Thy fair example may we trace,
To teach us what we ought to be.
