July 11

Godly Fear a Source of Joy

Scripture

KJV

Blessed is every one that feareth the Lord; that walketh in his ways. For thou shalt eat the labour of thine hands: happy shalt thou be, and it shall be well with thee.

ESV

A Song of Ascents. Blessed is everyone who fears the LORD, who walks in his ways! You shall eat the fruit of the labor of your hands; you shall be blessed, and it shall be well with you.

Psalm 128:1-2

Poem

Happy, beyond description, he
Who fears the Lord his God;
Who hears his threats with holy awe,
And trembles at his rod.
