December 18

Praises of Heaven

Scripture

KJV

They sung a new song, saying, Thou wast slain, and hast redeemed us to God by thy blood out of every kindred, and tongue, and people, and nation.

ESV

And they sang a new song, saying, “Worthy are you to take the scroll and to open its seals, for you were slain, and by your blood you ransomed people for God from every tribe and language and people and nation.

Revelation 5:9

Poem

Hark! hark! the voice of ceaseless praise
Around Jehovah's throne,
Songs of celestial joy they raise,
To mortal lips unknown.
