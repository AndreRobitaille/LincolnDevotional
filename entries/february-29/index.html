February 29

The Witness of Conscience

Scripture

KJV

And hereby we know that we are of the truth, and shall assure our hearts before him. Beloved, if our heart condemn us not, then have we confidence toward God.

ESV

By this we shall know that we are of the truth and reassure our heart before him; Beloved, if our heart does not condemn us, we have confidence before God.

1 John 3:19, 21

Poem

How happy are the new-born race,
Partakers of adopting grace!
How pure the bliss they share!
Hid from the world and all its eyes,
Within their hearts the blessing lies,
And conscience feels it there.
