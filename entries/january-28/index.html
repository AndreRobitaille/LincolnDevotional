January 28

Delivered from All Enemies

Scripture

KJV

He delivereth me from mine enemies; yea, thou liftest me above those that rise up against me.

ESV

Who rescued me from my enemies; yes, you exalted me above those who rose against me; you delivered me from the man of violence.

Psalm 18:48

Poem

Foes are round us, but we stand
On the borders of our land:
Jesus, God's exalted Son,
Bids us undismayed go on:
Onward then we gladly press
Through this earthly wilderness.
