October 26

Retrospection Should Lead to Self-examination

Scripture

KJV

I have considered the days of old, the years of ancient times. I call to remembrance my song in the night: I commune with mine own heart: and my spirit made diligent search.

ESV

I consider the days of old, the years long ago. I said, “Let me remember my song in the night; let me meditate in my heart.” Then my spirit made a diligent search.

Psalm 77:5-6

Poem

Help me, O Lord, to try my heart,
To search with strictest care,
And all my thoughts, and words, and ways,
With Scripture to compare.
